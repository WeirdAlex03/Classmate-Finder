Welcome to the future home of Classmate Finder!

Releasing Spring 2022


What is Classmate Finder?

Classmate Finder is a tool to help you find and reach out to your classmates before classes start. Fill out your classes, invite all your friends to do the same, and then use this to find everyone who shares a class with you!


How will it work?

Once it's ready, you will be able to create an account and add some contact information, then fill out a web form with a list of all the classes you will be taking in the upcoming semester.

Once you have filled out the form, you'll be able to view everyone in your classes, alongside their contact info so you can get together before classes start.


Why do I want to use this?

If you have a group of friends and you want to know what classes you have in common, then instead of everyone sharing screenshots of their schedules and going through everyone else's screenshots to look for shared classes, everyone can just fill this out and have the work be done for them!


Are there any integrations?

Potential integrations that may be added include:

  • Email notifications when someone new is in your class
  • Discord bot to fill out information and get notifications
  • Maybe similar bots on other platforms if there is demand
  • For University at Buffalo students only - potential integration with hUB to automatically import data
    • This depends on there being an API that I can use for this and I don't know if that exists. I have to look into that

What's next?

Some potential features that won't be included in the initial release, but may come later include:

  • Adding past classes you've taken so you can advise future students
  • Listing yourself as willing to be a mentor for students needing help in a class
